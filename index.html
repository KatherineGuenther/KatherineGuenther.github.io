Welcome to the Yachats Fireworks Committee Website

In 2001, the Yachats Fireworks Committee was formed for the sole purpose of blowing stuff up on the Fourth of July. We are an all-volunteer organization and rely almost exclusively on our generous fans for funding.

Don't Want to Make a Contribution?

That's okay. We're always looking for extra help on the day of the event. Check out the opportunities to participate as a volunteer.
